What to Expect

Your Tour,
Step by Step

Here's exactly what happens from the moment you arrive to when you leave.

1

Welcome at Reception

Meet your dedicated tour guide at the Main Reception, Block A. Tea and refreshments provided.

2

Campus Walkthrough (45 min)

Guided tour of classrooms, labs, library, sports complex, canteen, and student council room.

3

Meet a Teacher (15 min)

A brief informal Q&A with a faculty member from the department most relevant to your child's grade.

4

Admissions Desk (15 min)

Sit down with our admissions coordinator to ask questions about the process, fees, and availability.

5

Take Home a Brochure

Leave with a full information pack — prospectus, fee structure, academic calendar, and transport routes.

FAQs

Common Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your visit.

Do I need to bring anything for the tour?

Just your booking reference number and a valid photo ID. We'll have everything else ready for you. Children are welcome and encouraged to come along.

Can I reschedule after booking?

Yes, you can reschedule up to 24 hours before your tour by calling our admissions desk at +07.08722, 43710 or replying to your confirmation email.

Is the tour available during school holidays?

Tours run on most school days. During summer and winter breaks, we offer special holiday tour batches. Availability is shown in the slot selector when you book.

Will I be able to speak to teachers during the tour?

Yes — every tour includes a 15-minute informal Q&A session with a teacher from the department most relevant to your child's grade level.

Can I book a tour for multiple children?

Absolutely. Just mention the grades of all children when filling the form under "Grade Applying For" and our admissions team will ensure the tour covers relevant departments for all of them.

Prefer to Talk First?

Our admissions team is available Monday to Saturday, 9AM – 5PM.
