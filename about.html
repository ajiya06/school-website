Message from the Principal

Education is not the
filling of a pail, but
the lighting of a fire.

"At Global Academy, we do not merely teach subjects — we cultivate human beings who think deeply, act ethically, and lead courageously."

When we opened our doors in 2005, we made a promise — that every child who walks through our gates would leave better equipped for life than when they arrived. Not just in academics, but in character, in resilience, and in empathy.

Twenty years on, I am proud to say that promise has never wavered. Our teachers are not just educators — they are mentors who know each student by name, by strength, and by dream. Our classrooms are not just rooms — they are spaces where curiosity is celebrated and questions are welcomed.

To every parent who trusts us with their child: thank you. To every student who walks our corridors: you are the reason we come to work every day with joy.

Harish Bhatt
Principal & Founder Trustee
Core Values

The Six Pillars We Stand On

These aren't words on a wall — they are woven into every lesson, every interaction, every decision we make.

01

Intellectual Curiosity

We nurture a love of learning that goes beyond textbooks — encouraging students to question, explore and discover independently.

02

Integrity

We teach students to be honest in their words, fair in their actions, and consistent in their values — even when no one is watching.

03

Compassion

Empathy is a skill. We build it deliberately — through community service, peer mentoring, and a culture of genuine kindness.

04

Excellence

We celebrate effort as much as outcome — pushing every student to reach their personal best without compromising their wellbeing.

05

Responsibility

From classroom duties to environmental stewardship, we develop students who understand that with freedom comes accountability.

06

Global Perspective

We prepare students for a connected world — fostering cultural appreciation, digital literacy, and a mindset that transcends borders.
