A Day in the Life

What Does a Typical Day Look Like?

From the morning bell to the final farewell — here's how a student's day unfolds at Global Academy.

7:45 AM

Morning Yoga & Assembly

Students begin with 20 minutes of yoga and mindfulness, followed by the school assembly, announcements and thought of the day.

Wellness
8:15 AM

Academic Sessions — Block 1

Three 45-minute periods covering core subjects. Smart classroom tools keep lessons interactive and engaging.

Academic
10:45 AM

Short Break

A 20-minute refreshment break — students visit the canteen or relax in the courtyard before the next block.

Break
11:05 AM

Academic Sessions — Block 2

Lab work, group projects, or language sessions. Senior grades often have free study periods for board preparation.

Academic
1:00 PM

Lunch Break

45 minutes of free time — students eat in the canteen, visit the library, or head to the playground for informal games.

Lunch
1:45 PM

Activity & Club Period

Every student attends their chosen club — debate, coding, music, art, eco club or sports practice. No academics this hour.

Co-Curricular
2:45 PM

Sports & Physical Education

Outdoor sports, athletics training, yoga, or PE class. Participation is mandatory for all grades — active body, active mind.

Sports
3:45 PM

Home Time & Bus Departure

School ends at 3:45 PM. GPS buses depart in staggered batches. After-school library access available until 5:00 PM.

Dismissal

GPS-Tracked Fleet

All 10 buses have live GPS tracking. Parents can view real-time bus location through the school app.

Trained Drivers & Attendants

All drivers are police-verified with 3+ years experience. Every bus has a trained lady attendant for primary students.

CCTV on All Buses

Interior cameras with footage stored for 30 days. Access available to parents on verified request.

24/7 Transport Helpline

Dedicated transport coordinator available during school hours. Emergency helpline active until the last bus reaches home.

ID Card Entry System

Students scan their school ID to board the bus. Any deviation from the usual stop triggers an automatic parent notification.
