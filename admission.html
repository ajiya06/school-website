FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about admissions at Global Academy.

What is the minimum age for admission to Grade 1?

The child must have completed 6 years of age as of 31st March of the admission year. This follows CBSE guidelines for age eligibility.

Is there an entrance test for all grades?

Grades 1 and 2 have an informal interaction session. For Grades 3 and above, a written aptitude test in English and Mathematics is conducted on campus.

Can I apply for mid-year admission?

Mid-year admissions are considered on a case-by-case basis subject to seat availability. Please contact the admissions office directly for assistance.

What streams are available in Grade 11?

We offer three streams: Science (PCM / PCB), Commerce, and Humanities (Arts). Stream allocation is based on Grade 10 board results and student preference.

Are scholarships or fee concessions available?

Yes! We offer merit-based scholarships for students scoring above 90% in their previous grade. Sibling discounts of 10% are also available.

How can I track my application status?

Once submitted, you'll receive an Application ID by email. Use this ID on our website's "Track Application" page or call our admissions helpline.

Is school transport available?

Yes, we operate a GPS-tracked bus service covering major city areas. Transport fees are charged separately. Routes and schedules are shared upon enrolment.

Still Have Questions? We're Here to Help.

Our admissions team is available Monday to Saturday, 9AM – 5PM.
